Top 5 Dream Destinations

  1. Kyoto, Japan
    • Visit ancient temples & shrines
    • Watch cherry blossoms
    • Try local Kaiseki cuisine
  2. Bali, Indonesia
    • Relax on beaches and sunsets
    • Visit rice terraces & temples
    • Try water-sports/adventure
  3. Paris, France
    • Tour museums & Eiffel Tower
    • Food tour — boulangeries & patisseries
    • Walk the Seine at night
  4. New Zealand (South Island)
    • Hike Milford Track
    • Explore fjords and glaciers
    • Go bungee or adventure sports
  5. Rome, Italy
    • Visit Colosseum & Roman Forum
    • Savor authentic Italian gelato & pasta
    • Explore Vatican City
